# USBR rope-access anchor research corpus

This package is a manually audited, text-only qualitative digest from two allowed public Bureau of Reclamation bodies: the official project page for project 6390 and the two-page research bulletin *Testing and Verifying Rope Access Anchors*.

The package is marked `direct_training_ready: true`. Both exact first-party bodies were normally accessible and checksumable. Eligible agency-authored prose is treated as a United States federal public-domain work. Numerical results, configurations, procedures, products, brands, photographs, standards and legal text, equipment, contact details, and future operational tests are excluded.

The protected final report identified as Document 1018 is a hard exclusion. It was not requested or accessed, and no package claim infers, reconstructs, or quotes it.

Files:

- `CORPUS.md`: claim-cited, explicitly labeled non-bondage rope-access digest.
- `sources.jsonl`: exact source identity, retrieval, rights, and checksums.
- `surfaces.jsonl`: manual decisions for each material HTML surface.
- `dispositions.jsonl`: one manual decision for each bulletin PDF page.
- `components.jsonl`: component and controlled-block exclusion audit.
- `source_snapshot/provenance.json`: retrieval, PDF metadata, rights, and protected-document boundary.
- `REPORT.md`: review method, retained observations, and exclusions.
- `manifest.json`: schema identifiers, readiness flag, statistics, and artifact hashes.
- `tests/test_corpus.py`: deterministic integrity, scope, and leakage checks.

Neither allowed source body is redistributed. Their official URLs and exact checksums are recorded for independent verification.
